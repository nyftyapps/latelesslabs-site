1. Introduction

GRHS Consulting LLC ("Nyfty Apps") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information for the Lateless app.

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data solely for providing and maintaining App functionality, responding to your support inquiries, and improving App performance. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for targeted advertising.

Under the NJDPA and other global privacy laws, you have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion. You can initiate a deletion request within the App settings or by emailing our support team. You may also opt-out of non-essential data collection at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party SDKs to help perform tasks on our behalf. These partners are obligated not to disclose or use your information for any other purpose.
